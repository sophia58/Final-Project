Kenya Primary School Education Enrollments

The following data shows education enrollments for each county in Kenya. The data is separated by two groups: girls vs boys - from Grades 1 through 8.

How to Use This Map

Use the slider to view totals from Grades 1 to 8. You can hover over each circle marker to get information on the county, number of boys vs girls enrollment for the year, and to view a trend for each group from Grades 1 to 8.
